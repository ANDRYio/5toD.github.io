Juan Pablo Duarte

Founding Father of the Dominican Republic

Biography

Juan Pablo Duarte was born on January 26, 1813, in Santo Domingo. He was one of the main leaders in the movement for the independence of the Dominican Republic.

La Trinitaria

In 1838, Duarte founded a secret society called La Trinitaria. Its goal was to achieve independence from Haitian rule and create a free Dominican nation.
